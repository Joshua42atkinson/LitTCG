## What is Word Slimes?

**Word Slimes** is the anti-flashcard. Learners don't memorize vocabulary — they **spell words to summon collectible pets**, then use their SemanticSlime companion in tactical Wand Duels and grammar quests.

Spell **"inferno"** and get a fiery, angry Rare pet. Spell **"serenity"** and get a calm, glowing Support pet. Every word's meaning becomes the pet's element, stats, face, and combat moves. No two words produce the same creature.

The same word changes its effect depending on the Slime's emotional face — so learners discover that meaning is contextual, not just definitional.

## How It Plays

- 🌟 **Spell & Summon** — Collect letter crystals and arrange them into real English words. Press Submit. Watch the card flip and the pet burst out.
- ⚔️ **Wand Duel Combat** — Use your SemanticSlime in 1v1 battles. Play synonyms for heavy attacks. Play antonyms to counter/block enemy attacks. Master semantic relationships.
- 🏛️ **Help NPCs with Scenario Quests** — Fill Mad-Lib slots tied to language subjects: simple past, negation, tone, active voice, and more. Grammar and pragmatics through play, not worksheets.
- 🎴 **Collect, Evolve, Master** — Mastered words and your Slime gain XP, evolve, and unlock new districts.
- 🐾 **Choose a Companion** — One pet follows you through the world, reacting to the words you meet.
- 🎵 **Procedural Music** — The soundtrack shifts as you explore, battle, and grow.
